About Me

I grew up on a small farm in the outskirts of Mexico City. I love the farm life, it was hard work waking up at 4 in the morning daily to feed the animals and get the water up and running but everyday was an adventure. I remember chasing chickens, being chased by chickens and being kicked by a bull (yes it hurt!). We immigrated to the US when I was 9 years old. I remember being blown away! So much conveniences and technology! When I found out about Disney land, ohhh ma gaaaad! I did my best at school and I was selected to go to Boston for a leadership camp at Mount Herman. I never could have imagined the journey I was set on but I can say that it all goes back to my farm out in Mexico and I do my best to try to express what I see with Art.
